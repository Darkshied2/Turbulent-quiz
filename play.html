Rule of the game

  1. There are 13 questions that must be answered correctly to win 10,000 coins
  2. Players will pass through 5 game level points, where the higher the level the more difficult the questions given
  3. On the way to the 10,000 coin ladder, players will be provided with 3 options for help, namely 50/50 (Fifty fifty), Highlight answer (highlight answers), and Googling (googling answers).
  4. If the Player answers incorrectly, then the coins obtained are in accordance with the position on the coin ladder at that time

    The game will end if and only if:

  1. Player answered incorrectly
  2. The player has correctly completed the 13th question.
